    About Us

    In 1997, a group of friends formed a welfare organization to support one another, the Welfare group was formally registered as Ndunyu Self Help Group in 1999. As a Self-help Group, members started by contributing Kshs.5/- every day. By 2008, due to continued growth in membership, savings portfolio, loans portfolio and Associated risks and legal restrictions limiting self-help groups’ membership, the group was Advised to transform to a Sacco.

    In 2010 AGM the group finally converted to Sacco registered as HOME BUSINESS SAVINGS & CREDIT COOPERATIVE SOCIETY LIMITED. Formal application for registration was made in September 2011 and registration certificate issued in November 2011, in the name of Home Business Sacco Savings & Credit Cooperative Society Limited.

    In March 2012, members’ shares in Ndunyu Self-help Group were transferred to Home Business Sacco. And in October of the same year, members resolved to allow the Management committee prepare the ground for FOSA operations.
